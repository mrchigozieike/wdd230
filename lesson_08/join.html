NonProfit Members

No fee

Non profit memberships are for businesses that are not for profit enterprizes and are not self-employed.

Membership programs are a fantastic way to cultivate focused support for your cause and generate predictable revenue.

Bronze Membership

$30/month

Bronze membership is for businesses with less than 50 employees that are for profit.

Benefits include: access to the O-town Chamber of Commerce network, attend training events and discount on advertisement postings..

Silver Membership

$80/month

Silver membership is for businesses with less than 250 employees that are for profit.

It includes the same all the benefits of the bronze membership and in addition spotlight on our home page, and access to exclusive chamber events and conferences.

Gold Membership

$120/month

Gold membership is for businesses with more than 250 employees that are for profit.

It includes all the benefits of silver membership and the opportunity to serve on the Board of Directors. In addition of access to Chamber experts, and feedback.
